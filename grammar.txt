<S> ::= <text> <S'>
<S'>::= nl <S> | EPS
<text> ::= str | italic <text> italic | bold <text> bold

terminal symbols:
str: [a-zA-Z]+
italic: **
bold: ''
nl: \n
EPS: empty string


<S> ::= <line> <S'>
<S'>::=  nl <S> | EPS
<line>::= <text> <textlist>
<text> ::= str | <format> <text>
<textlist> ::= <format> <textlist> | EPS
<format> ::= italic <text> italic | bold <text> bold

Final Grammar

<S> 		::= <TextSegment> <MoreText>
<MoreText> 	::= <TextSegment> <MoreText> | nl <TextSegment> <MoreText> | ε
<TextSegment> 	::= text <TextTail> | italic <TextTail> | bold <TextTail>
<TextTail> 	::= italic <TextTail> | bold <TextTail> | ε
